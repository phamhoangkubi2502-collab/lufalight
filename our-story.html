The Story Behind Lufalight

Founded in Pain.
Purpose. Light.

For over twenty years, our founder lived the life the world called success — boardrooms, international travel, long hours, endless pressure. Behind it came chronic pain, poor sleep, inflammation, and the quiet toll no résumé shows. This is the story of how that changed.

Hugh
Founder, Lufalight
The Turning Point

By his late-40s, Hugh no longer felt like himself. He was beginning to get hooked onto sleeping pills and painkillers. Rather than accepting this as inevitable, he searched for a science-backed solution to reclaim his health — not a quick fix, something real, backed by evidence. That search led him to red and near-infrared light therapy.

He approached it the way he approached everything in business: rigorously. He studied the research, tested devices, tracked his own results week over week.

What happened next changed everything.

The pain that had shadowed his mornings began to lift. Sleep returned — deep, restorative sleep. His energy came back. His skin began to change. The transformation wasn't just physical — it was a reclaiming of self. And Hugh thought: if this worked for me, it has to reach more people.

Why Lufalight Exists

Not a marketing brief. A journey.

LUFALIGHT was born from that journey — from lived pain, real healing, and an unshakeable belief in what light, the right wavelengths applied with precision and regularity, can do for the human body.

Hugh set out sourcing the most advanced manufacturing partners, demanding the highest certifications from every one of them, and spent the next five years in prototyping and testing until the science and the product were both undeniable.

Today, LUFALIGHT exists to give families, friends, and customers the same springboard Hugh found: a comprehensive, integrated line of science-backed light therapy devices designed to support recovery, healthy aging, and long-term vitality.
