FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

How does referral tracking work? +
Each approved ambassador receives a unique referral link and promo code. Every order placed through your link is automatically attributed to your account. You can monitor your clicks, conversions, and commission balance at any time through your personal ambassador dashboard.
When and how are commissions paid out? +
Commissions are calculated monthly and paid on the 15th of the following month via Interac e-Transfer (Canada) or PayPal (international). There is a 30-day order hold before commissions are released — this aligns with our 30-day return window. Minimum payout threshold is CA$50.
Can I apply if I live outside Canada or the USA? +
Yes — the Ambassador Program is open internationally. Our LPQS scoring system already accounts for 30+ countries. Commissions are paid in CAD via PayPal for international ambassadors. Note that the Audi R8 prize experience takes place in Vancouver, Canada; travel logistics will be arranged for international winners. Return airfare is capped at a maximum of $1,000 CAD for all winners, whether within Canada/USA or international — any amount above this cap is the responsibility of the winner.
What happens if my score is below 50 (Waitlist)? +
A Waitlist result means your profile is not quite ready yet — typically due to audience size or engagement rate. You are welcome to grow your platform and re-apply after 6 months. Our team will send you specific feedback on which scoring categories to focus on. Many of our strongest ambassadors started on the Waitlist.
Can I move up to a higher tier after joining? +
Absolutely. All ambassador tiers are reviewed annually. If your platform has grown significantly — more followers, higher engagement, or stronger sales performance — you can request a re-evaluation at any time. Tier upgrades are automatic for top performers at each annual review.
Can I be both a Wholesale Partner and an Ambassador? +
Yes, the two programs are separate and compatible. Wholesale Partners purchase inventory at volume pricing for resale, while Ambassadors earn referral commissions on sales through their unique link — no inventory required. Many clinic owners and practitioners participate in both. Apply to whichever fits your business model, or both.
Is there a minimum sales requirement to stay active? +
There is no hard monthly minimum. However, ambassador accounts with zero activity for 6 consecutive months may be moved to inactive status and their referral link deactivated. Gold and Diamond tiers have an optional quarterly sales target that unlocks bonus incentives — hitting these is encouraged but never mandatory.
