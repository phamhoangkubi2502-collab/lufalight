1. Acceptance & Parties

This Ambassador Program Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Lufalight Inc. ("Lufalight", "we", "us") and the individual or entity submitting an ambassador application ("Ambassador", "you").

This Agreement is effective upon the earlier of: (a) submission of your ambassador application, or (b) your written or electronic acceptance of an offer to join the program. It supersedes any prior verbal or written discussions about ambassador participation.

2. Eligibility

To be eligible for the Lufalight Ambassador Program you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age.
  • Have an active, publicly accessible social media profile, professional website, or business presence.
  • Not be an employee, director, or immediate family member of a Lufalight Inc. employee or director.
  • Not be under any contractual obligation that prevents you from promoting Lufalight products.
  • Comply with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction, including advertising disclosure requirements (FTC guidelines for US-based ambassadors; ASC guidelines for Canadian ambassadors; equivalent rules elsewhere).

Lufalight reserves the right to decline any application at our sole discretion without obligation to provide a reason.

3. Application & LPQS™ Score

Applications are evaluated using the LPQS™ (LufaLight Partnership Qualification System), a proprietary 100-point scoring algorithm that assesses six categories: social media reach, professional background, geographic market, audience engagement rate, content quality, and brand alignment.

  • The LPQS score generated during your application is a preliminary score based on information you self-reported.
  • Lufalight reserves the right to verify all information provided. Misrepresentation of follower counts, profession, engagement rates, or any other material information is grounds for immediate rejection or termination.
  • Lufalight may request supporting documentation (e.g., screenshot of analytics dashboard, professional licence) before confirming your tier.
  • A preliminary score does not constitute a binding offer or guarantee of acceptance.

4. Ambassador Tiers

Accepted ambassadors are assigned to one of three tiers based on their confirmed LPQS score. Each tier carries a base commission rate (see Section 5), a maximum accelerated commission rate (see Section 5a), and a one-time Demo Credit Wallet (see Section 5b):

  • 💎 Diamond (90–100 points, or by direct invitation): 15% base commission, up to 18% with the Performance Accelerator. CAD $2,000 Demo Credit Wallet, 60-day loan period, CAD $15,000 sales threshold to keep demo products.
  • 🥇 Gold (70–89 points): 10% base commission, up to 13% with the Performance Accelerator. CAD $1,000 Demo Credit Wallet, 60-day loan period, CAD $10,000 sales threshold to keep demo products.
  • 🥈 Silver (50–69 points): 5% base commission, up to 8% with the Performance Accelerator. CAD $500 Demo Credit Wallet, 45-day loan period, CAD $5,000 sales threshold to keep demo products.

Applicants scoring below 50 points are placed on a Waitlist and may reapply after 6 months. Tier-specific benefits are described on the Ambassador Program page and may be updated from time to time in accordance with Section 13.

5. Commission & Earnings

Lufalight will pay you a commission on eligible orders generated through your unique referral link or promo code, subject to the following:

  • Base commission rates are published by tier in Section 4 above, and may increase automatically under the Performance Accelerator described in Section 5a.
  • Commissions are calculated on the net order value (excluding taxes, shipping, and any discounts applied).
  • An order is "eligible" once it is shipped, paid in full, and the 30-day return window has expired without a return or chargeback.
  • Commissions are not earned on: your own purchases, purchases by immediate family members, orders placed using any discount stack not expressly approved by Lufalight, or orders that are subsequently refunded, charged back, or cancelled.

5a. Performance Accelerator

Your commission rate increases automatically as your cumulative verified net sales grow within a program year (January 1 – December 31), up to the maximum rate for your tier:

  • Diamond: +1% at CAD $50,000 in sales, +2% at CAD $100,000, +3% at CAD $200,000 (maximum 18%).
  • Gold: +1% at CAD $30,000, +2% at CAD $75,000, +3% at CAD $150,000 (maximum 13%).
  • Silver: +1% at CAD $15,000, +2% at CAD $40,000, +3% at CAD $80,000 (maximum 8%).

Accelerated rates apply prospectively to orders placed after the applicable threshold is reached, and reset at the start of each new program year unless otherwise communicated by Lufalight in writing.

5b. Demo Credit & Product Loan Program

Upon acceptance, each ambassador receives a one-time Demo Credit Wallet (amount per Section 4) redeemable for any combination of eligible Lufalight products at retail value, shipped at Lufalight's expense.

  • Demo products remain the property of Lufalight for the duration of your tier's Loan Period (45 or 60 days from the shipment date).
  • If you generate verified referral sales equal to or greater than your tier's Sales Threshold (Section 4) before the Loan Period ends, the demo products become yours to keep permanently at no additional cost.
  • If the Sales Threshold is not met by the end of the Loan Period, you may (a) return the demo products to Lufalight in like-new, resalable condition at your own shipping cost, or (b) purchase them at Lufalight's then-current ambassador wholesale price. Lufalight may, at its sole discretion, grant a one-time extension of up to 30 days.
  • The Demo Credit Wallet is a one-time benefit issued upon initial tier qualification. It is not reissued on tier renewal, except that a tier upgrade entitles you to the difference between your new and previous tier's Demo Credit amount.

5c. Ambassador Referral Rewards

You may refer new ambassadors to the program. A referral reward is paid once your referred ambassador is approved and generates at least CAD $2,500 in verified referral sales within their first 90 days as an active ambassador:

  • Silver referral: CAD $100
  • Gold referral: CAD $250
  • Diamond referral: CAD $500

The reward amount is based on the referred ambassador's confirmed tier at the time the CAD $2,500 threshold is reached. There is no limit to the number of ambassadors you may refer. Referral rewards are paid on the next regular payout date following confirmation and are subject to the same eligibility and anti-fraud provisions as ordinary commissions (Section 9).

5d. Milestone Rewards

In addition to tier commissions and the Performance Accelerator, ambassadors of any tier unlock the following one-time rewards as cumulative verified sales within a single program year reach each milestone, subject to product and inventory availability:

  • CAD $5,000 — Lufalight branded apparel package
  • CAD $10,000 — CAD $100 product credit
  • CAD $25,000 — CAD $500 travel/marketing credit
  • CAD $50,000 — VIP product launch invitation
  • CAD $100,000 — Elite Ambassador recognition
  • CAD $250,000 — Executive retreat invitation (subject to availability)

6. Payment Terms

  • Commissions are calculated monthly and paid on or around the 15th of the following month (e.g., January commissions paid by February 15th).
  • Minimum payout threshold: CA$50. Balances below this threshold roll over to the next month.
  • Payment methods: Interac e-Transfer (Canadian ambassadors) or PayPal (international ambassadors). You are responsible for any fees charged by your payment provider.
  • You are solely responsible for reporting and paying any taxes on commissions earned. Lufalight will issue a T4A (Canada) or equivalent statement if required by applicable law.
  • Commissions are paid in Canadian Dollars (CAD). International ambassadors accept currency conversion risk.
  • Lufalight reserves the right to withhold payment pending investigation of suspected fraud or policy violations.

7. Referral Tracking & Attribution

  • Each ambassador receives a unique referral link and/or promo code. Attribution is based on the last-click model with a 30-day cookie window.
  • If a customer uses your promo code at checkout but did not click your referral link, the order is still attributed to you.
  • Only one ambassador may receive commission per order. In the event of conflicting attribution, Lufalight's internal records are final.
  • You must not share your promo code in a manner designed to generate discount-seeking traffic unrelated to genuine product promotion (e.g., coupon aggregator sites, cashback portals) without prior written approval.
  • Lufalight may modify the tracking system at any time. We will notify active ambassadors of material changes with at least 14 days' notice.

8. Brand & Content Guidelines

When creating content that mentions or promotes Lufalight, you agree to:

  • Disclose the relationship clearly and conspicuously in every piece of sponsored or commission-generating content, using language such as "#ad", "#sponsored", "#LufalightPartner", or equivalent per applicable advertising standards.
  • Only make claims about Lufalight products that are accurate, substantiated, and consistent with information on our website. You may not make medical claims (e.g., "cures", "treats", "prevents disease") not expressly authorized in writing by Lufalight.
  • Use only approved brand assets (logos, product images, copy) provided in your ambassador marketing kit. Modified or outdated brand assets may not be used without approval.
  • Not create or publish content that is defamatory, discriminatory, sexually explicit, or that could harm the Lufalight brand reputation.
  • Submit content for pre-approval if required by Lufalight in writing for a specific campaign.

9. Prohibited Conduct

The following are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate termination and forfeiture of unpaid commissions:

  • Placing orders through your own referral link or code for the purpose of earning commissions (self-referral fraud).
  • Creating fake reviews, fake social media accounts, or artificially inflating engagement metrics.
  • Bidding on Lufalight's brand keywords in paid search advertising without prior written approval.
  • Misrepresenting your relationship with Lufalight (e.g., claiming to be an employee or official representative).
  • Promoting competing red light therapy products during the term of this Agreement without prior written consent.
  • Sharing, selling, or transferring your referral link or promo code to another person or entity.
  • Using spam, unsolicited messages, or deceptive landing pages to drive traffic.
  • Violating any applicable law in connection with your ambassador activities.

10. Intellectual Property

Lufalight grants you a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable licence to use Lufalight's trademarks, logos, product images, and other brand materials ("Brand Assets") solely for the purpose of promoting Lufalight products in accordance with this Agreement.

  • You may not alter, distort, or combine Brand Assets with other elements in a way that misrepresents Lufalight.
  • You retain ownership of original content you create. By tagging or submitting content to Lufalight, you grant Lufalight a royalty-free licence to repost, share, and use that content for marketing purposes with appropriate credit.
  • This licence terminates automatically upon termination of this Agreement.

11. Audi R8 Vancouver Competition — Official Rules

Competition Overview

The top 3 Lufalight Ambassadors worldwide ranked by total net sales generated through their referral links during the Competition Period — August 1, 2027 to December 31, 2027 at 11:59 PM Pacific Time — will be awarded the Vancouver Experience Prize ("Prize"). The challenge is open to ambassadors of every tier (Diamond, Gold, and Silver); your starting tier does not affect your chances of winning.

The Prize

  • Round-trip airfare to Vancouver, Canada (economy class, from nearest major airport), capped at a maximum of $1,000 CAD per winner — this cap applies equally to winners within Canada/USA and international winners. Any airfare cost above $1,000 CAD is the responsibility of the winner.
  • 4 days / 3 nights luxury hotel accommodation (Lufalight's choice of property)
  • Supervised drive experience in the Lufalight-branded Audi R8 V10
  • Professional brand video shoot featuring the winner and the Audi R8
  • Professional photography session
  • Lufalight Global Ambassador Trophy
  • Feature on LufaLight.com and Lufalight's official social media channels
  • VIP invitation to the Annual Ambassador Summit

The Prize has no cash equivalent and is non-transferable. Lufalight will coordinate scheduling with each winner. Travel must be completed by December 31, 2028.

Ranking & Tie-Breaking

Rankings are based on confirmed net sales (gross sales minus returns and chargebacks) attributed to each ambassador's referral link or promo code during the Competition Period. In the event of a tie, the ambassador who reached the tied sales figure first (by date/time of the qualifying order) ranks higher.

Eligibility for the Prize

  • Winners must be in good standing (no policy violations, no pending investigations) at the time the Competition Period ends.
  • Winners must be willing to participate in a brand video shoot and photography session, and grant Lufalight rights to use the resulting footage and images for marketing.
  • Winners are responsible for any personal taxes arising from receipt of the Prize in their jurisdiction.
  • Lufalight employees, contractors, and their immediate family members are ineligible.

Automatic Diamond Promotion (Top 3 Challenge)

Any Gold or Silver Ambassador who finishes in the Global Top 3 automatically receives Diamond Ambassador status — including the Diamond commission rate, Demo Credit Wallet, and all other benefits described in Section 4 — for the following program year, in addition to the Prize. Existing Diamond Ambassadors remain eligible for the Prize but receive no additional promotion, having already attained the program's highest tier.

Competition Modifications

Lufalight reserves the right to modify, suspend, or cancel the competition in the event of circumstances outside our reasonable control (force majeure, regulatory changes, safety concerns). In such cases, Lufalight will provide written notice to all active ambassadors and will make reasonable efforts to honour an equivalent prize or pro-rated recognition.

Privacy & Winner Announcement

Winners consent to having their name, social media handle, and general location (city/country) announced publicly by Lufalight. Full address and contact details will not be published. Winners may opt out of public announcement by notifying Lufalight in writing before the prize is awarded.

12. Term, Suspension & Termination

This Agreement begins on the date your application is accepted and continues until terminated by either party.

Termination by you:

You may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing written notice to lufalight@gmail.com. Commissions earned and confirmed prior to the termination date will be paid on the next regular payout date.

Termination by Lufalight:

Lufalight may terminate this Agreement immediately and without prior notice if you violate any provision of Section 9 (Prohibited Conduct) or make material misrepresentations. For other breaches, Lufalight will provide 14 days' written notice and an opportunity to cure.

Inactivity:

Accounts with zero attributed sales for 6 consecutive months may be moved to inactive status, deactivating your referral link. You may request reactivation at any time.

Effect of termination:

Upon termination, your referral link is deactivated, your licence to use Brand Assets is revoked, and you forfeit any commissions earned through prohibited conduct. Legitimate accrued commissions above the minimum threshold will be paid within 60 days of termination.

13. Tier Reviews & Program Changes

Ambassador tiers are formally reviewed once per year, typically in January, following the conclusion of the annual Competition Period (Section 11). Reviews may result in a tier upgrade, tier downgrade, or no change based on updated LPQS scoring, sales performance, and content quality.

Automatic Diamond Promotion (Annual Top 10)

Independently of the Top 3 Challenge described in Section 11, any Gold or Silver Ambassador who finishes within the Global Top 10 in annual net sales rankings is automatically promoted to Diamond Ambassador for the following program year, effective January 1. This promotion includes the Diamond commission structure, CAD $2,000 Demo Credit Wallet, and all other Diamond benefits described in Section 4. Existing Diamond Ambassadors remain eligible for all prizes, commissions, bonuses, and recognition under this Agreement, but are not eligible for additional Diamond promotion, having already attained the program's highest tier.

Lufalight may modify commission rates, tier benefits, or program rules at any time with 30 days' written notice to active ambassadors. Continued participation after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the updated terms. If you do not accept the changes, you may terminate this Agreement without penalty within the 30-day notice period.

14. Privacy & Data

By applying and participating, you consent to Lufalight collecting and processing your name, email address, social media handles, phone number (optional), country, and application responses for the purpose of operating the ambassador program.

We do not sell your personal data. Your information is shared only with service providers necessary to operate the program (e.g., payment processors, email platform). Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing lufalight@gmail.com, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records (e.g., payment records for tax purposes).

15. Disclaimer & Limitation of Liability

The Ambassador Program is provided "as is". Lufalight does not guarantee any particular level of commissions, traffic, or earnings. Past performance of other ambassadors is not indicative of future results.

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, Lufalight's total liability to you under this Agreement shall not exceed the total commissions paid to you in the 3 months preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Lufalight is not liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.

You agree to indemnify and hold harmless Lufalight Inc., its officers, directors, and employees from any claims, losses, or expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising from your violation of this Agreement, your ambassador activities, or your content.

16. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Province of British Columbia, Canada, and the applicable federal laws of Canada, without regard to conflict-of-law principles.

The parties agree to first attempt to resolve any dispute informally by contacting Lufalight at lufalight@gmail.com. If informal resolution fails within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration in Vancouver, British Columbia under the rules of the ADR Institute of Canada, with proceedings in English. Nothing in this clause prevents either party from seeking emergency injunctive relief in a court of competent jurisdiction.

17. Amendments

Lufalight may amend this Agreement at any time. Material changes will be communicated by email to your registered address with at least 30 days' notice. Non-material changes (e.g., clarifications, formatting) take effect upon publication. The current version of this Agreement is always available at lufalight.com/ambassador-terms.html.

18. Contact

For all ambassador program inquiries, commission disputes, or questions about this Agreement:

  • Email: lufalight@gmail.com
  • Program page: lufalight.com/ambassador.html

By submitting your application you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Ambassador Program Agreement in its entirety.
