FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Red light (630–660nm) and near-infrared (810–1064nm) penetrate skin tissue to activate mitochondria, increasing ATP (cellular energy) production. This process — called Photobiomodulation (PBM) — is validated by over 5,000 peer-reviewed studies.

Muscle recovery benefits can be felt after the first session. Skin and hair growth results typically require 4–8 weeks of consistent use (10–20 min/day). Best results after 8–12 weeks of regular sessions.

All Lufalight devices meet ISO 13485 and IEC 60601 medical safety standards. PBM is FDA-classified as safe (non-thermal, non-ionizing). Do not use if pregnant, have a history of skin cancer, or are taking photosensitizing medications.
